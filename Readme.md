# 📊 Unemployment Analysis with Python

## CodeAlpha Data Science Internship - Task 2

This project was developed as part of the **CodeAlpha Data Science Internship**. The objective is to analyze unemployment trends in India using Python, explore the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on unemployment rates, and generate meaningful insights through data visualization and exploratory data analysis (EDA).

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Unemployment is one of the most important economic indicators that reflects the health of a country's economy. During the COVID-19 pandemic, unemployment rates increased significantly due to lockdowns and business closures.

This project performs a comprehensive analysis of unemployment data to identify trends, compare unemployment rates before and during COVID-19, and discover patterns that can support policy-making and economic planning.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Clean and preprocess the unemployment dataset.
- Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
- Analyze unemployment trends across different states.
- Study the impact of COVID-19 on unemployment rates.
- Identify monthly and regional unemployment patterns.
- Create informative visualizations.
- Generate insights that can support economic and social policy decisions.

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset used in this project is obtained from **Kaggle**.

It contains information such as:

- State
- Date
- Frequency
- Estimated Employed
- Estimated Unemployment Rate (%)
- Estimated Labour Participation Rate (%)
- Region

## 🔍 Key Findings

- COVID-19 significantly increased unemployment rates across India.
- Several states experienced much higher unemployment than the national average.
- Monthly unemployment trends revealed clear fluctuations during lockdown periods.
- Labour participation showed variation across different regions.
